The Snackpass Story

Our journey began as students at Yale- where we wanted to make ordering at local restaurants more convenient, affordable and fun with friends.

WE built the Snackpass app, a social mobile ordering app for restaurants - order ahead, earn loyalty points, and connect with friends by gifting loyalty points, foods, and even hatch chickens:)

The Snackpass app has gone viral on college campuses across the country from Yale to UC Berkeley to Machigan and more. As we spent years working in and directly with our restaurant partners, we realized there was more we could do to help QSR operators succeed.

QSR technology today is outdated, fragmented and expensive. We reimagined the QSR technology stack from the ground up to be modern, elegant and spark joy in every customer touch point

We believe every restaurant should have access to enterprise-grade innovation and technology, without needing to spend millions of dollars.

Our mission is to bring world-class technology, previously reserved for Fortune 500s, to all QSR operators

Kevin, Jaime, and Snackpass team.
